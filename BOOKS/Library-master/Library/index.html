    Frequently Asked Questions

    • Can we only get books in original?

      No. You can get books in two ways. Either in original or in a pdf format.

    • What to do if the book isn't available?

      Most of the books are available here. still,if by chance the book you want isn't available, then you can either wait for some days or send a request to a person owning the book at present.

    • Can we share books in bulk(as of a whole semester or year)?

      Yes, you can do that for sure. Just go to the share section and mention the year or semester and the stream(if specified-Science,Commerce or Arts) in the description box ,and your ready to go!

    • How is the quality of the pdf we will download?

      So, it basically depends on the quality of the pdf the user shares. However, many a times you will find multiple options of the same book, so you can choose the most suitable one as per your choice.

    • Is it only for books or can we share and request other stuffs too?

      Presently the prototype is only for books. But very soon we are planning to add some more sections to it. Till then please stay tuned.
